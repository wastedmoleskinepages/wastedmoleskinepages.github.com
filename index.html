Wasted Moleskine Pages
About

They're beautiful books. Really beautiful. So beautiful that every now and then a page of doodling, sketching or thinking doesn't really fill the page to the potential that was needed.

To make you feel a little less guilty we started Wasted Moleskine Pages. Group therapy for the page wasters.
